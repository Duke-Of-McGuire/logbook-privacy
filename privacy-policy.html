LogBook Privacy Policy

Your data, your business. Here's what we collect and why.

Effective: February 21, 2026

Overview

LogBook ("we," "our," or "the app") is a personal logging app that lets you track and share activity with friends. We take your privacy seriously and collect only what's necessary to make the app work. We do not sell your data to third parties. Period.

Information We Collect

When you use LogBook, we collect the following types of information:

üìß Account Information

Your email address, username, and chosen avatar when you create an account. This is stored securely via Firebase Authentication and Firestore.

üìç Location Data

üìù Log Entries

The entries you create, including timestamp, type, comments, bathroom rating, and location. These are stored in your personal account on our servers.

üë• Social Features

What we do NOT collect: We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, browsing history, contacts, photos, health data, financial information, or any data beyond what is listed above. Firebase Analytics is explicitly disabled in our app.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide app functionality:

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To store and display your log entries
  • To enable sharing with groups you choose to join
  • To power the group chat feature
  • To display your entries on a map and calendar

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond delivering the app's features.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore), which provides encryption in transit and at rest. Access to your data is controlled by security rules that ensure only you can read your personal logs, and only group members can access shared content.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is only visible to:

  • Group members ‚Äî only logs you explicitly choose to share are visible to members of that group.

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time.
  • Modification: Update your username and avatar in your profile.
  • Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from the app via Profile ‚Üí Delete Account. This removes your user record, username, and authentication credentials. This action is irreversible.

Children's Privacy

LogBook is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
